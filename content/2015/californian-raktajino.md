Title: Californian Raktajino
Date: 2015-12-02 05:00
Status: published
Category: asides
Tags: recipes
Slug: californian-raktajino

_Ingredients:_

* 1/2 packet [Starbucks Via Instant Sweetened Iced Coffee powder](http://store.starbucks.com/starbucks-via-iced-coffee-011006899.html)
* about 16 fl. oz. [Soylent 1.4](https://www.soylent.com/) (refrigerated, prepared some hours previously)
* "a couple" fl. oz. tap water

[![californian_raktajino1](http://zackmdavis.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/californian_raktajino1-216x300.jpg)](http://zackmdavis.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/californian_raktajino1.jpg)

_Instructions:_

<ol>
<li>Pour coffee powder in bottom of glass.</li>
<li>Add enough water to dissolve the coffee powder; stir.<br>
<a href="http://zackmdavis.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/californian_raktajino2.jpg"><img src="http://zackmdavis.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/californian_raktajino2-196x300.jpg" alt="californian_raktajino2"></a></li>
<li>Pour Soylent. Soylent will mostly sink below the coffee; stir with a somewhat vertical motion until color is uniform.<br>
<a href="http://zackmdavis.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/californian_raktajino3.jpg"><img src="http://zackmdavis.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/californian_raktajino3-208x300.jpg" alt="californian_raktajino3"></a>
<a href="http://zackmdavis.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/californian_raktajino4.jpg"><img src="http://zackmdavis.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/californian_raktajino4-220x300.jpg" alt="californian_raktajino4"></a>
<a href="http://zackmdavis.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/californian_raktajino5.jpg"><img src="http://zackmdavis.net/blog/wp-content/uploads/2015/11/californian_raktajino5-204x300.jpg" alt="californian_raktajino5"></a></li>
<li>Today is a good day to live! Drink; enjoy; move fast; break things!</li>
</ol>
